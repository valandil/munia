Input adapters

Capture videogame inputs
Show your inputs on screen or stream!

Videogame input adapters can capture gamepad inputs and allow to them to be used for purposes such as playing in emulators, displaying them on your screen, capturing them for display on twitch, making video tutorials, or even mapping them to be used on unrelated videogame consoles.

Console modifications
Get the most out of your consoles

Most older and some recent consoles can be modified to expand their capacbilities. Mods can have purposes ranging from gaining the ability to run homebrew applications to improving the video output quality or adding new output methods entirely.
